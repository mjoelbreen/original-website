About Me

I am a digital content creator based in New Orleans, specializing in video editing/production, photography, writing, graphic design and 3D modeling.

The rise of the digital economy has led not only to the disruption of industries outside the digital world, but also many within in. This is why I find it important to be skilled in many forms of media production.

I have:

  • a bachelor's degree in English Writing from Loyola University New Orleans
  • edited music videos and performances at WWOZ FM in New Orleans
  • experience using non-linear editing software, like Adobe Premiere and Edius
  • experience with Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator
  • spent plenty of time with 3D objects using Blender as well as animating and programming them in Unreal Engine

I currently work at WWL-TV in New Orleans.
